Description

Armani Si Passione Perfume by Giorgio Armani, Armani si passione is a sensual, romantic fragrance perfect for evening wear. Top notes of pear, blackcurrant and pink pepper create a juicy, spicy opening impression. Heart notes of rose, jasmine and heliotrope build a lush floral center, supported by soft, full base notes of cedarwood and vanilla.
